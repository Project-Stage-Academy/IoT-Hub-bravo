# Meetings Schedule

## Daily Standup
- Duration: 10-15 minutes
- Owner: team Lead
- Time: 10:00 AM - 04:00 PM
- Format: short status (Yesterday, Today, Blockers)

## Weekly Demo & Retro
- Duration: 30-60 minutes
- Owner: team Lead
- Time: Friday
- Format: demonstrate completed features, review progress, discuss issues and solutions

## Sprint Planning
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Owner: team Lead
- Time: first day of sprint
- Format: discuss upcoming user stories, prioritize tasks, assign work, and clarify requirements
